Local Wise
Local Wise is a web-based application developed as part of my thesis project. Built using ASP.NET, this platform serves as a comprehensive hub connecting tourists with local tour guides in various cities around the world. The primary objective is to facilitate seamless communication and collaboration between tourists seeking authentic local experiences and certified tour guides offering their services.

The platform employs a user-friendly interface that allows for easy navigation and interaction. Tourists can browse through various cities and explore the profiles of different tour guides before making a selection. Likewise, tour guides can manage their profiles, update their offerings, and respond to inquiries from potential tourists.

My Road Map
Travel Planner is a mobile application developed to provide a personalized and intelligent travel experience. Built using Expo and integrated with the powerful ChatGPT from OpenAI, this application is an innovative tool for those looking to plan their trips conveniently and effectively.

The main functionality of Travel Planner is the creation of tailor-made travel itineraries, all through a simple interaction with the chatbot powered by ChatGPT. Users can start by typing the desired destination in the search bar, and then ChatGPT takes action, suggesting must-see locations, exciting activities, and even recommendations for restaurants and hotels based on their personal preferences.

TaskiFy
Taskify is a mobile application designed to streamline task management for users on the go, offering a convenient solution for organizing daily activities. Developed using the Expo platform, Taskify offers a user-friendly interface and robust features to help individuals organize their daily activities effortlessly.

The core functionality of Taskify revolves around creating, tracking, and managing tasks effectively. Users can easily add new tasks to their lists, specifying details such as due dates, priorities, and additional notes to ensure clarity and organization. Whether it's a work assignment, a personal errand, or a project milestone, Taskify provides the flexibility to adapt to various task types and requirements, ensuring users can stay on top of their tasks with ease.
